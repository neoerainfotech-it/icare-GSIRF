What is GSIRF?

About GSIRF

GSIRF is a pioneering state-level institutional rating initiative developed by the Knowledge Consortium of Gujarat (KCG) — an Education Department initiative of the Gujarat Government — in partnership with ICARE (Indian Centre for Academic Rankings & Excellence), India's most trusted institution ranking and rating authority.

The framework employs a highly credible and transparent methodology wholly based on objective data. ICARE audits, assesses, and rates institutions with a clear focus on outcomes — helping academic leaders identify areas of improvement and achieve excellence at regional, national, and global levels.

6 Core Evaluation Parameters

Every institution is evaluated across six comprehensive parameters covering all dimensions of higher education quality.

Teaching & Learning

Quality of curriculum, pedagogical practices, faculty qualifications, student-teacher ratios, and learning outcomes.

Research & Innovation

Publications, patents (IPR), sponsored research projects, research funding, and innovation ecosystem strength.

Infrastructure

Physical campus, laboratories, library resources, digital infrastructure, sports and welfare facilities.

Student Support & Outcomes

Placements, higher education progression, scholarships, student grievance mechanisms, and holistic development programmes.

Governance & Leadership

Institutional governance structure, leadership quality, IQAC functioning, accreditation status, and strategic planning.

Sustainability & Social Responsibility

Green campus initiatives, financial sustainability, community engagement, and social responsibility activities.

Meet GARVI — The Analytics Portal for GSIRF

GARVI

Powered by GSIRF

GARVI (Gujarat Academic Ratings Visualization for Improvement) is the official analytics and data intelligence platform built on top of GSIRF data. It transforms raw institutional data into powerful, actionable insights.

Using GSIRF data as its backbone, GARVI gives institutions three powerful tools — Analytics, Benchmarking, and a Simulator — to understand their standing, compare with peers, and model the impact of strategic decisions.
